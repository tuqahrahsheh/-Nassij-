About Us

Naseej - For Sustainable Services

Established: 10th February 2020

Commercial Registration Number: (245584), registered under the Trade Names Law No. 9 of 2006.

Vision:

To lead and excel in building the Jordanian community and enhancing individuals' capabilities in a creative and stimulating environment. This is achieved through empowerment, guidance, promoting social solidarity, and ensuring sustainable and purposeful development. The institution aims to adopt forward-looking goals and aspirations with proper planning, execution, and involvement of individuals and communities.

Mission:


To enhance human skills development across all governorates of Jordan by designing beneficial programs for the community, especially targeting youth as the cornerstone and future leaders. The institution focuses on embracing and attracting pioneering ideas, encouraging volunteer work, and promoting community engagement.
